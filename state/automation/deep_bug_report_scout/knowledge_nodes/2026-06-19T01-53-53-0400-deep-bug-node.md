## Root Lesson
- id: deep-bug-root-2026-06-19-occupant-facility-pressure
- statement: Occupant, facility/economy, and resource-pressure kits need immutable config, stable identity, finite transaction, and restored-state invariants before they can become promoted operations-domain surfaces.
- why it matters: Current public APIs can preserve spawn-rule runtime timing across reset, generate duplicate occupant ids, commit non-finite facility transactions into economy accounts, and expose contradictory depletion state.
